Shahar Varon - My Military Journey

Fighter in Sayeret Matkal

I started my military journey as a fighter in Sayeret Matkal, one of the most elite special forces units in the IDF.

Transition to Maglan

After serving in Sayeret Matkal, I transitioned to Maglan, where I continued to develop my skills as a fighter in another prestigious unit.

Officer Training and Commanding Roles

Upon completing officer training, I served as a commander in the 101st Battalion, then returned to Maglan. My leadership skills were further honed during this period.

Bachelor's Degree in Economics

In between my military service, I pursued and completed a bachelor's degree in economics, which provided me with valuable insights into the world of finance and management.

Company Commander in Maglan and the 202nd Battalion

After completing my degree, I returned to the army as a company commander, first in Maglan and then in the 202nd Battalion, where I led and mentored the next generation of soldiers.
