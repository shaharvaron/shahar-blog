7.10 - The Beginning

It all started on 7.10, a day that marked the beginning of a challenging journey. The preparations for the first entry were intense, filled with anticipation and uncertainty.

The First Entry

The first entry was a critical moment. It was a mixture of adrenaline, focus, and the unknown. We moved forward with the mission, knowing that this was just the beginning.

The Confrontation and Injury

During the first entry, we faced a sudden confrontation. The situation escalated quickly, leading to my injury. The pain was immediate, but the mission had to continue.

The Return

After the injury, I returned to regroup. It was a time for reflection, healing, and preparing for what was to come. The second entry loomed on the horizon, and I knew I had to be ready.

The Second Entry

The second entry was even more intense. We were more prepared, but the stakes were higher. Every move was calculated, and every decision carried weight.

The Third and Final Entry

The third and final entry marked the culmination of this journey. It was the last push, the final hurdle. The challenges were immense, but so was the determination to succeed.

Transition to Product Management

After the last entry, I knew it was time for a new chapter. The transition to product management was a natural progression. It allowed me to take the skills and experiences I had gained and apply them in a new, challenging environment.
