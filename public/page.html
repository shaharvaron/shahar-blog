Shahar Varon - Before the Army

Pre-Military Fitness Program

Before joining the army, I was part of a rigorous pre-military fitness program. This program helped me build the physical and mental toughness needed to succeed in one of the most challenging units in the IDF.

Accepted to Sayeret Matkal

Through hard work and determination, I was accepted into Sayeret Matkal, one of the most elite special forces units in the world. This achievement is a testament to my dedication and perseverance.

Extensive Travels Around Israel

In addition to my military preparation, I also took the time to travel extensively around Israel. I explored the country's beautiful landscapes, from the deserts of the south to the mountains in the north, gaining a deep appreciation for the land I was preparing to defend.
